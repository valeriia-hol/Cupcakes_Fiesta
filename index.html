Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize my cupcakes for specific dietary preferences?

Absolutely! We offer customization options to accommodate various dietary preferences, including gluten-free, vegan, and nut-free alternatives. Simply let us know your preferences during the ordering process.

Can I include a personalized message with my cupcake order?

Absolutely! During the checkout process, you'll have the opportunity to include special instructions, including personalized messages. We'll make sure to add that special touch to your order.

Are there options for large orders or events?

Certainly! We specialize in catering to events of all sizes. Explore our "Custom Cupcakes" section for personalized options, and don't hesitate to reach out to our support team for assistance with large or bulk orders.

What payment methods do you accept?

We offer convenient payment options, including credit/debit card payments through our secure online checkout. Additionally, we provide a cash-on-delivery option for those who prefer to pay in person.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

While we strive to fulfill orders promptly, same-day delivery availability depends on order volume and your location. We recommend placing your order in advance, but feel free to contact our support team for urgent requests.

Do you ship outside the city?

Currently, we focus on serving our delicious cupcakes within the city limits. For inquiries about shipping to areas outside our usual delivery zone, please contact our support team to discuss potential arrangements.
